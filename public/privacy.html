Privacy Policy

Slidememos - Side Panel Notes

Summary: Slidememos stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal information.

Data Collection

Slidememos does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Browsing history
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Crash reports or diagnostics

Data Storage

All data created in Slidememos (notes, memos, settings) is stored exclusively using:

  • Chrome Storage API (chrome.storage.sync and chrome.storage.local)
  • IndexedDB (for search functionality)

This data remains on your local device and is subject to your browser's data management policies. If you use Chrome sync, your data may be synchronized across your devices through your Google account.

External Communications

Slidememos makes external network requests only in the following scenarios:

  • URL Metadata Fetching: When you save a URL, the extension fetches the webpage to extract its title and icon. This is done directly from your browser to the target website.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions solely for functionality:

  • sidePanel: To display the notes interface in the browser side panel
  • activeTab: To quickly capture the current page URL
  • storage: To save your notes locally
  • scripting: To extract metadata from web pages you choose to save

Third-Party Services

Slidememos does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

Effective Date: February 3, 2025

Slidememos is an open-source project. Your privacy is our priority.
